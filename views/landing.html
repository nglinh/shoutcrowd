Vocal Real-Time™ Gaming

Shoutcrowd brings real-time gaming to a whole new level

This is not just another real-time gaming platform. Here at shoutcrowd, we embody the philosophies that the whole is more than the sum of its parts. As a shoutcrowd player, you take part in a collective decision process with millions of others. However there is a caveat because we believe that creativity always loves constraint. The biggest novelty of shoutcrowd lies in our unique rules of engagement — your voice. We call it, Vocal Real-Time™ Gaming (VRTG). Welcome to the future of boisterous gaming with the crowd!
